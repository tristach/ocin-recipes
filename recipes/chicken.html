Rosemary Chicken

Description

Put chicken breast, thighs, or bascially anything in a pan. Cover it with salt, pepper and rosemarry and bake it. It's not rocket science dumb-dumb, it's dinner:).

All joking aside, this is a family favorite. The fresh rosemary gives it a really rustic flavor and it couldn't be easier.

For add-ons, simply throw carrots, onions, potatoes, anything you've got really into the pan and let it come along for the ride.

Works well with pork, beef, lamb, etc. too.

Ingredients

  • chicken (stupid)
  • rosemary (maybe you didn't see the name?)
  • salt (duh..)
  • pepper (even more duh.....)
